Alyanna Posadas

Alyanna Posadas is a hobbyist baker and cook. Starting from childhood, her love for food and cooking came from her dad. Growing up with Asian flavors and living in a western country, she found that the easiest way to feel connected to her heritage was through food. Alyanna cooks nearly every day and loves experimenting. Her favorite things to cook are anything BBQ or cookies. Alyanna is also the creator of this website.


Why "Love, Yan" ?










"Love, Yan" is my way to immortalize my family's version of classic Filipino recipes through a food recipe website. After years of talking about writing down the family recipes, they're now stored in the form of this website. Part of this was for me and my brother, so we have an online reference for our childhood foods, but this also serves as a thank you to my parents for giving us both our love for food and cooking. Hence, the title of the website, "Love, Yan".
